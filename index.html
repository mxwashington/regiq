RegIQ - Real-Time Regulatory Intelligence Platform

AI-powered monitoring of FDA, USDA, EPA, and CDC regulatory alerts for compliance professionals.

Core Features

  • Instant FDA recall and safety alert notifications
  • USDA food safety monitoring and FSIS alerts
  • EPA enforcement tracking and regulatory updates
  • CDC outbreak and health advisory monitoring
  • Smart categorization and urgency scoring
  • Custom alert filtering and search capabilities
  • Mobile-first design for field teams
  • Enterprise integration APIs

Target Industries

RegIQ serves quality assurance teams, regulatory affairs professionals, and compliance managers in:

  • Food & Beverage: Supply chain monitoring, ingredient safety, facility inspections
  • Pharmaceuticals: Drug recalls, FDA warnings, manufacturing alerts
  • Agriculture: Pesticide regulations, feed safety, livestock alerts
  • Consumer Goods: Product safety recalls, labeling requirements

Why RegIQ

Traditional regulatory monitoring requires manual checking of multiple agency websites. RegIQ automates this process, delivering critical alerts in real-time through an intuitive dashboard that works on any device.
